Music

Rhythm
Rhythm is everywhere — in movement, in relationships and in life.

Years ago in Ukraine, while attending a professional music program, I discovered my passion for drums, and began to understand the cadences of music and energy, and how much they affect us.

Artistry
Artists push consciousness forward.

Art adds flavor to culture. In 2020, I founded a music label, WiseTribe, to record and promote the talents of brilliant world musicians at our studio in San Diego.

Joy

At the end of the day, music enhances my experience of life. It opens my creativity, increases my happiness, and reminds me of the central rhythms of energy and existence.
